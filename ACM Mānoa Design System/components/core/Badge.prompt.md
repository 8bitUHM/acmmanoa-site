**Badge** — a rounded-full tinted pill. The site uses the `brand` variant for leadership role labels ("President", "Faculty Sponsor") and section chips.

```jsx
<Badge>President</Badge>
<Badge variant="neutral">Workshop</Badge>
<Badge variant="solid">New</Badge>
```

- Always `--radius-full`. Padding is 0.5rem × 1rem.
- `brand` = blue-50 bg / blue-600 text (the default everywhere on the site).
