The Space

ICSpace.

An interactive classroom dedicated to the ICS community at the University of Hawaiʻi at Mānoa.

POST 318B

Welcome to ICSpace, an interactive classroom dedicated to the Information and Computer Sciences (ICS) community at the University of Hawaiʻi at Mānoa. Located in POST 318B, this facility is designed exclusively for ICS undergraduate majors, graduate students, faculty, and staff. It fosters an environment of learning and collaboration.

Guests are also welcome, provided they are accompanied by an ICS community member, ensuring a broad exchange of ideas and knowledge.

What it is

A hub for innovation and learning.

Essential for advancing goals

ICSpace is essential for advancing the educational and professional goals of the ICS community. It serves as a central hub where students and faculty can thrive.

  • Students and faculty can engage in innovative projects
  • Cutting-edge research can be conducted
  • Interactive learning sessions can be held

Advanced technology & resources

The space is equipped with advanced technology and resources that are crucial for preparing students for the demands of the tech industry. It enhances hands-on learning experiences and research capabilities, bridging the gap between academic knowledge and real-world applications.

The presence of ICSpace not only enhances the academic experience but also significantly boosts the attractiveness of the ICS program to prospective students and faculty.
